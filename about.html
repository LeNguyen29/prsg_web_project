ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome To PRSG

PRSG is a fan made MMORPG game on Roblox that aims to deliver the most authentic premium Power Rangers and Super Sentai experience that it can. The game takes inspiration from various RPG games and features an open world map with story driven quests.

Developed by Fragmented Studio

Development began in 2021 as small game project, driven by a group of passionate fans who have long dream of an RPG style Power Rangers game. Since then, the project has went through numerous challenges, along with multiple iterations and reimaginings, until a clear creative vision is reached that defines the game today.

KEY FEATURES

MORPHING

Morph into your favorite Ranger with show-inspired transformations, animations, and visual effects. Choose from a wide selection of suits spanning generations of Power Rangers and Super Sentai teams.

KEY FEATURES

ROLL CALL

Annoucing yourself after morphing is a long standing tradition in both Power Rangers and Super Sentai. In the game, you and your friends can take part in that tradition, recreate iconic synchronized team roll calls straight from the shows.

KEY FEATURES

RANGER ABILITIES

Every Ranger comes equipped with their own signature weapons and abilities, inspired by the shows and comics. With a wide variety of playstyles and powers to choose from, there is something for every type of player.
